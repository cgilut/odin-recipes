Orc Vitality Drink recipe:

The Lord of the Rings: The Orc Vitality Drink.

"Ugluk thrust a flask between teeth and poured some burning liquid down his throat: he felt a hot fierce glow flow through him. The pain in his legs and ankles vanished. He could stand."

- The Two Towers, Chapter 3

Time to prepare and cook: 2 minutes

Yield: 1 Drink

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz sambuca
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 1 1/2 oz absinthe

Instructions:

  1. Combine, stir, and enjoy. At first impression, this drink smells of licorice but goes down incredibly sweet and smooth.
